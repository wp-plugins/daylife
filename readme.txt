Add high quality licensed images relevant to your blog posts from sources like Getty, AP, Reuters and US Presswire.

== Description ==

**About the Plugin:** The [Daylife](http://daylife.com/ "Daylife - Simply Amazing Cloud Publishing") Plugin helps you find relevant images to insert into your posts while writing your post in the editor. The plugin will suggest you images relevant to the text of your post as you are editing it, or let you find Images by searching for a keyword. Once you like an image, you can mouse over the image, and insert it into your blog post.

**Image Licenses and pricing:** This plugin helps you find licensed images from sources like Getty, AP, Reuters and more. See the complete list of all the content partners [here](http://www.daylife.com/about-us/our-partnerships/). To learn more about these Image Licenses and their pricing, drop an email to [getdaylife@daylife.com](mailto:getdaylife@daylife.com).

**Getting Started:** Please drop an email to [getdaylife@daylife.com](mailto:getdaylife@daylife.com), and they will set your Plugin Settings - your AccessKey, SharedSecret and a Source Filter with access to your licensed sources.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the files to the /wp-content/plugins/daylife/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
